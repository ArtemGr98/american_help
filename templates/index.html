What is happening in Ukraine?
Russia invaded Ukraine on 24 February 2022, a major escalation of the Russo-Ukrainian War. Russia went to war against Ukraine, killing more than 2,000 people in just one week. The Russian army is targeting civilians, their homes, their lives, their future. Many Ukrainians are using subway stations as bomb shelters and civilians have been rushing to evacuate to other countries. Children continue to be killed, wounded and deeply traumatized by the violence all around them. Families are terrified, in shock, and desperate for safety. Many cities in Ukraine are destroyed such as Mariupol and Kharkiv.
